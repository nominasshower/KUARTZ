About us

The first quartz slab factory in Mexico.

Founded in 2019, Küartz Surfaces joined the group and became the first and only quartz slab factory in Mexico. Located in Juárez, Nuevo León, our 75,348 ft² plant produces slabs, vanity tops, kitchen countertops, quartz walls and floors with specialized machinery.
