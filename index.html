Material benefits

Engineered for real life.

Natural quartz is one of nature's hardest materials. Our slabs are engineered consisting of 95% quartz mineral along with resin made up of polymers and pigments. Since it is a man-made material, it can come in a broad variety of colors and textures.

Scratch and stain resistant
Low maintenance surface
Resistant to household chemicals
Excellent workability — custom cuts & shapes
Non-porous — hygienic and food-safe
Impact resistant
Fire resistant — non-combustible
Easy to clean

Care & Maintenance

Küartz is a non-porous material characterized by its hardness and high resistance to stains, making it one of the easiest surfaces to maintain. Unlike natural stone, it requires no sealing.

For everyday cleaning, use a soft cloth with warm water and mild soap. Avoid abrasive pads, bleach-based cleaners, and prolonged exposure to direct heat above 150°C.

⚠
Do not place hot pots or pans directly on the surface. Always use trivets or heat pads to protect the finish.
